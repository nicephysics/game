A Phyzicz Game

A Phyzicz Game is a simple physics game about stars, planets and asteroids.

Brief Aim

The player controls a simple Spherical Flying Object (SFO). The aim of the game is to protect as many planets as possible from incoming asteroids, by hitting them out of the planet's atmosphere.

Stars and Planets

Planets orbit around stars, and are where the action of the game take place. The player controls the SFO to shoot projectiles that hit away asteroids flying towards planets out of the planet's atmosphere, preventing damage to its surface. Each planet has lives that can be deducted if asteroids hit its surface. The amount of lives a planet has is specific to it. Each star has varying amounts of planets orbiting around it. To unlock stars and their planets, the player has to pass the asteroid waves of previous planets. See more on stars and planets here!

Asteroids

Asteroids have varying types, masses, sizes, and shapes. Because of their different masses, they can fly towards the planet as different speeds. There are different types of asteroids of different difficulties. Some asteroids has a turret attached to it, aiming at the player's SFO. If the spaceship gets hit, it will get stunned! The amount of lives deducted from planets depends on the type of asteroid that hit its surface. See more on asteroids here!
