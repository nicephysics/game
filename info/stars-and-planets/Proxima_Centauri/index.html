Proxima Centauri (star)

Proxima Centauri is the first star in the game.
It is a tutorial star, containing 2 tutorial planets: Proxima Centauri b and Proxima Centauri c.

Planets

Planet Description
Proxima Centauri b In this planet, the player gets introduced to the game!
The tutorial introduces the basic elements of gameplay to the player: the controls and asteroids.
Proxima Centauri c The second part of the tutorial!
This tutorial introduces health, points, levels, upgrading properties and upgrading to a new class.
